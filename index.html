Silvopasture

Bringing trees and livestock together for a greener future

What is Silvopasture?

Silvopasture is a smart way of farming that mixes trees, animals, and pasture together. It helps farmers use land wisely, keeps animals healthy, and supports nature at the same time.

Benefits of Silvopasture

✔️ Improves soil health
✔️ Increases biodiversity
✔️ Provides shade and shelter for livestock
✔️ Generates additional income from trees

How It Works?

Farmers plant trees on grazing lands, allowing livestock to roam freely. The trees provide shade and protection while enhancing soil fertility. Over time, both plants and animals thrive together.
